Commands:
- Movement: n, s, e, or w | moves you in that direction. Example: to go North, type 'n' and press <enter>
- Communicating: say <msg> | talks to people you can see in the same room as you. Example: say hi there
- Getting Stuff: get <item> / drop <item> | picks up an item from the room or drops an item from your inventory.
- Inventory: inventory | lists the items in your inventory.
- Quitting: quit | logs you off
